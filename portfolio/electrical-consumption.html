Electrical Energy Consumption and Popultion

This map was made as part of Geography 370, Introducation to Cartography. For this task we were given the prompt to produce a map for the FixWikiMaps Initiative which is a collaborative project to publish open access map resources high cartographic quality to Wikipedia. This map had to also be a proportional symbol map. I chose to create a map to show the unequal consumption of electrical energy around the world compared to the overall population of the country. No suprise, most of the top energy consuming countries have a much higher average electrical consumption per individual person that lives in that county compared to the rest of the world.
