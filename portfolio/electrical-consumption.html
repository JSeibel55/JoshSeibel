Electrical Energy Consumption and Population

This map was made as part of Geography 370, Introduction to Cartography. For this task we were given the prompt to produce a map for the FixWikiMaps Initiative which is a collaborative project to publish open access map resources with high cartographic quality to Wikipedia. This map had to also be a proportional symbol map. I chose to create a map to show the unequal consumption of electrical energy of various countries compared to the overall population within each country. Unsurprisingly, most of the top energy consuming countries have a much higher average electrical consumption per individual person that lives in that country compared to the rest of the world.
