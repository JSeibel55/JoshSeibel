U.S. National Parks

I had wanted to make a map like this ever since I started visiting the US National Parks. Both to celebrate all of the National Parks around the country but also to help myself keep track of all of the ones I've been to. I've been lucky enough to visit 28 of these parks so far and work for one of them (Grand Teton NP). Ultimately, I made this specific map as a gift for my significant other.
