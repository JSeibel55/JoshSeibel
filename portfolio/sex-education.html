Sex Education: Does Abstinence-Only Education Work?

This map was made as part of Geography 572, Graphic Design in Cartography. For this task we were asked to create a long-form infographic that goes beyond a single information graphic to tell a compelling visual story and expand the examples of the story that make use of advanced cartographic techniques, such as bivariate and multivariate mapping.
