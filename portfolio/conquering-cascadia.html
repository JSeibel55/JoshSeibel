Conquering Cascadia: Bike Race

This map was made as part of Geography 572, Graphic Design in Cartography. For this task, we were given the prompt to create promotional map for a pretend multi-day Intertional Champsionship Cycling Tour. The use of terrain mapping and hillshading was a primary goal of this project. With my love of the Pacific Northwest and having visited many of the places in this area, it was only fitting to create the race to criss cross these mountains. As scenic as this bike race would be to complete, I'll admite that some of these streches would be extremely unrealistic to have in a race.
