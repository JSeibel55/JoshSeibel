Wisconin's Breeding Bird Atlas: Breeding Birds

This map was made as part of Geography 370, Introduction to Cartography. For this task we were to create a choropleth map from a statistical dataset. Creating a map about bird conservation was an easy choice for me and this map has a personal connection as I have helped contribute data to this bird project! Through maps like this, it becomes much clearer to see how habitats and wildlife change over the years. With more long term data collection and analysis, biologists and legislatures can create the management plans needed to better preserve the habitats and organisms we share our world with.
